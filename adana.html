Explore Adana

Sabancı Central Mosque

5.0 420 ratings price: Free Mosque

One of the largest mosques in the Middle East, a modern architectural landmark by the Seyhan River.

Taşköprü (Stone Bridge)

4.8 260 ratings price: Free Bridge

Ancient Roman bridge still in use today, spanning the Seyhan River; symbol of Adana.

Adana Merkez Park

4.7 180 ratings price: Free Urban Park

The largest urban park in Turkey, ideal for walking, biking, and family time near the Seyhan River.
