FUN FACTS

Fresh updates and fun facts to inspire your next Turkish adventure.

First capital of the Ottomans

posted by admin april 2025

Before Istanbul, Bursa was the very first capital of the Ottoman Empire, rich in early Islamic architecture.

Longest cable car ride

posted by admin april 2025

The Bursa Teleferik stretches 9 kilometers up Uludağ Mountain — the longest cable car line in the world!

Birthplace of Iskender kebab

posted by admin may 2025

The delicious İskender kebabı — tender lamb slices over pita bread with yogurt and tomato sauce — was invented here!
