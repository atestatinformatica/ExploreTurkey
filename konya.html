FUN FACTS

Fresh updates and fun facts to inspire your next Turkish adventure.

City of Rumi

posted by admin april 2025

Konya is the final resting place of Mevlânâ Rumi — the world-renowned Sufi poet and philosopher, honored at the Mevlana Museum.

Whirling Dervishes

posted by admin april 2025

Konya is the spiritual home of the Whirling Dervishes — where the mesmerizing Sema ceremony takes place in honor of Rumi's legacy.

One of the world’s oldest cities

posted by admin may 2025

Konya’s history stretches back to Neolithic times — including Çatalhöyük, one of the earliest urban settlements in human history.
